About

My diverse background is an embodiment of my professional journey. I didn't quite know what I wanted to be, so when I graduated from my University, I joined a non-profit organization that focuses on mentoring college students.

Through this non-profit, I developed soft skills of active listening and communication that allowed me to thrive in a career in sales. However, I grew tired of being a cog in a machine and set out to be an entrepreneur

This lead me to specialize in marketing and real estate, which I run for my family business. As I focus on growing our company, I realized we needed to increase our digital presence. This eventually led me to Thinkful and the rest is history.
